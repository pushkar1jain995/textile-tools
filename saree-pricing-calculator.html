Aksar Pooche Jaate Sawaal
Saree pe kitna margin rakhna chahiye?
Fashion retail mein typically 50–70% margin rakha jaata hai. Cotton/synthetic pe 50–55%, silk pe 60–70%, designer/bridal pe 70–80% tak. Online selling pe platform commission ke liye extra 15–20% add karo.
Saree pe GST kitna lagta hai?
Saree aur fabric pe 5% GST lagta hai (HSN 5007/5208). Handloom sarees pe 0% GST hota hai. Embroidered ya readymade garments pe 18% GST lagta hai. CGST aur SGST barabar split hote hain intra-state sales mein.
Selling price mein GST include karna chahiye?
Haan — customer ko jo price dikhao usmein GST include hona chahiye. Agar aap GST registered hain toh aap Input Tax Credit (ITC) claim kar sakte hain purchase ke time pay ki GST pe.
Wholesale lot mein per piece cost kaise nikaalein?
Total lot cost mein shipping aur packaging add karo, phir total pieces se divide karo. Oopar "Multiple Sarees" calculator mein ye sab ek saath automatically calculate ho jaata hai.
